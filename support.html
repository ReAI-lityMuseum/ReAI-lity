Mission & Value

ReAI-lity Museum is a pioneering online institution dedicated to collaborative art between humans and AI. As the first museum of its kind, ReAI-lity seeks to showcase the potential of artificial intelligence in shaping our creative landscape and offering a thought-provoking glimpse into the AI-crafted worlds that challenge our perceptions of reality and fiction.

Based on the AI-Crafted Worlds, ReAI-lity Museum is dedicated to collecting, researching, and exhibiting AI-generated and AI-collaborated works as samples of our evolving society. We recognize the growing influence of artificial intelligence in shaping our creative landscape and seek to document and present these groundbreaking developments to the public.

ReAI-lity Museum actively partners with experts from diverse fields, including AI researchers, artists, technologists, scientists, and philosophers, to create dynamic and thought-provoking exhibitions and events. These collaborations foster a rich dialogue between different disciplines, enabling the museum to present a comprehensive and nuanced perspective on the impact of AI on art, society, and culture.
